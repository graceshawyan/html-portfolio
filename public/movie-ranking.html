Grace's Watch List

My top movies/shows of all time!

Spirited Away

I absolutely love Studio Ghibli films. The animations are lovely, and the music is beautiful. I watched this at camp with my COSMOS friends, so it was a core memory and favorite comfort movie!

“Once you've met someone you never really forget them. It just takes a while for your memories to return.” - Zeniba

Joy of Life

So many plot twists! This was definitely the best historical costume drama I watched, with an intriguing time-travel theme and incredible martial fight scenes. The sets and costumes are excellent, and it's pacing and comedy are top notch.

"Everyone wants to live again, more or less, because there are always too many regrets in life, so the real meaning of this story is to cherish the present and live for the good."

Sweet Home

I read the original Webtoon, so it was interesting to see the TV adaptation. It's an incredibly thrilling film that explores the depths of human nature. I love that this apocalyptic film made me think about what it means to be human.

"Even the blackest darkness, vanishes in the faintest light. Even the slightest chance, can give us hope." - Carnby Kim

Kaijou No. 8

An interesting anime I watched the summer before college. The humor is fantastic, and it's exciting to see the main character achieve his dream of becoming a defense force officer.

"Nervousness. Panic. I’m feeling all kinds of pressure. But for some reason… I’m also incredibly excited! – Kafka"
